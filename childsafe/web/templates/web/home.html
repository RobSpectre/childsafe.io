what is childsafe.io?

Kid-safe content made simple

childsafe.io protects your underage users from exposure to child pornography, CSAM and human trafficking.

Integrating with your favorite cloud storage provider, you can start scanning your user generated images from your website or mobile application against billions of images known to depict the exploitation of children. Comparisons are made through various hashing techniques and your images are never copied to disk, ensuring your content's privacy.

No code, no complexity, easy configuration in 5 minutes.
